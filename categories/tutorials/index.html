How Do You Invest Under 18?

How do you Invest as a Minor? If you’ve ever tried to sign up for a stock/crypto exchange, you’d know that they require Know Your Customer (KYC) verification. A KYC check’s purpose is to attempt to verify someone’s identity, and almost all major trading platforms/exchanges require it. You’ll be asked for things such as your SSN, driver’s license, passport, and possibly more. This makes it impossible for someone that’s under the age of 18 to create their own account.
